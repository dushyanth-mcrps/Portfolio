DELICIOUS PANCAKES

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour: 1 ½ cups (195g), spooned and leveled
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons (25g)
  • Baking powder: 1 tablespoon (12g), aluminum-free
  • Salt: ½ teaspoon (3g), reduce to ¼ teaspoon if sensitive to salt
  • Milk: 1 ¼ cups (295ml), dairy or non-dairy
  • Egg: 1 large
  • Unsalted butter: 5 tablespoons (70g), melted and cooled slightly
  • Vanilla extract: 2 teaspoons (10ml), reduce if sensitive to vanilla

Instructions

  1. Prepare the butter: Melt the butter and set it aside to cool slightly.
  2. Combine dry ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Combine wet ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Don't worry if the butter solidifies a little.
  3. Mix the batter: Create a well in the center of your dry ingredients. Pour the milk mixture into the well and gently whisk until the flour is just incorporated. A few small lumps are perfectly normal. As the batter rests, you should see it begin to bubble. It will be thick, like cake batter. If it seems too thick, add a splash more milk to thin it out.
  4. Preheat the skillet: Place a large skillet or griddle over medium heat. To check if it's ready, sprinkle a few drops of water on the surface. They should “dance” around and evaporate quickly.
  5. Cook the pancakes: Brush the skillet with melted butter for crispy edges (you can skip this if using a good nonstick pan). Scoop the batter onto the skillet using a 1/4 cup measure or a large cookie scoop, then gently spread each pancake into a 4-inch circle.
  6. Flip the pancakes: After 1 to 2 minutes, the edges will look dry, and bubbles will form and pop on the surface. Flip the pancakes and cook for another 1 to 2 minutes until they are lightly browned and cooked through. Enjoy your pancakes right away with warm syrup, more butter, and berries.
